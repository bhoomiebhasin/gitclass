### 🌆 The Vision
As our cities grow, the "Urban Heat Island" effect is becoming a critical climate challenge. We didn't just want to map the heat; we wanted to provide a **sustainability command center** for urban planners.

### ⚙️ How it Works (The "Magic" behind the Map):
*   **AI-Driven Micro-Zones**: Using **Google Gemini 3 Flash**, the app identifies specific thermal hotspots within a 5km radius.
*   **Real-World Grounding**: To eliminate AI "hallucinations," we cross-verify every zone against **OpenStreetMap** data. If the AI suggests a coordinate, we verify it’s a real neighborhood before you see it.
*   **Actionable Blueprints**: It doesn't just show problems; it gives solutions. From cool-roof coatings in dense slums to bioswales in industrial zones.
*   **Impact Metrics**: Real-time estimates for CO2 reduction and water absorption gains.

### 🛠️ The Build Experience
Building this in a high-intensity 2-hour sprint was an absolute adrenaline rush. We focused on building an **Anti-Hallucination Stack** using Python, Folium, and the new `google-genai` SDK to ensure that every sustainability recommendation was technically sound and geographically accurate.
